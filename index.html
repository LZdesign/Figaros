We are

A professional and friendly company where we aim to produce original pieces to suit your personal taste and environment and also restore old ones to their former beauty. Glass is a beautiful and versatile medium, there is a very large scale of different kind of glass and designs to choose from, we are always pleased to discuss a new idea or project and are looking forward to working with you.

WHAT WE DO

At Figaro's glass we restore and manufacture stained glass panels using traditional techniques.From small on site repairs to decorative panels for your home or business, no job is too small.We aim to create a pleasing environment using contemporary or traditional designs in order to suit your requirements and personal taste.We understand that with such a large variety available it can be confusing to decide on a single project, therefore, we are happy to take the time to help you achieve what is the best choice for you so feel free to contact us

CONTACT

The first step to take to discuss a project is to contact us by phone or email to arrange a first appointment. We will discuss your requirements or ideas and we’ll take it from there. On the next appointment we will discuss the developing of the design and glass samples. Installation can also be arranged. If a new piece is required for a present or a certain date, please keep in mind tha idea development of design andfabrication projects might take a few weeks to complete...with creativity, expertise and professionally.
We will arrange a first meeting when we will discuss your idea or the piece that needs to be restored. In case of a new project, a second meeting is recommended to discuss the development of the design and choice of glass. In case of restoration we will arrange a suitable time for the removal of the existing panel and a date for installation once the panel has been restored.
If you are interested in an existing piece, if still available, a viewing can be arranged. If you are interested in a reproduction of an existing piece, we can arrange a meeting to discuss your preferences in regards of glass and colours or any changes to suit your taste.
